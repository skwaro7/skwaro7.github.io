About me

My name is Marcin Skwarek, I come from Poland, I am 38 years old. I live there in a town called Zabkowice Slaskie, it's located in south west Poland , 60 km south of Wroclaw. I am running a recycling metal business with my father but I want to change carrer and become a digital nomad able to work remotly in 2 years. I have a beautiful daughter Maja, she is 3 years old, living with her mother. I love travelling, been to over 20 countries so far. For the previous 15 years I was a part time dj playing on different venues all around in Poland and also in Czech Republic. I am a foodie who ties his skills in the home kitchen from time to time.

Music

I was interested in music since I was a very young child. My parents were constantly listening to old school rock bands like Led Zeppelin, The Rolling Stones, The Beattles, Pink Floyd and also polish ones like budka Suflera and Perfect. I remember my first ever vinyl - Za Ostatni Grosz/For the last penny by Budka Suflera. Great song. When I was a teenager I discovered The Doors and I fall in love completly, at one moment in the past was able to sing almost all songs from memory. These were the times of great rock music bands like Nirvana, Pearl Jam, Soundgarden, Oasis, The Cranberries. I started to play drumms in my friends band. This is where the feel of rythm comes from, wich I would find very useful in the coming years. I started organizing electronic parties somewhere around 2000. These were parties usually deep in the forest, by the lake, where only invited people could come. Later on I started dj'ing myself and been doing this mostly for fun for the previous 15 years. I have been to many festivals, both rock and electronic music, such as: Love Parade, Mayday, Sunrise, Electric Kingdom, Open Er, Exit Festival, Woodstock, Rock am Ring.
